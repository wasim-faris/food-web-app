FREE DELIVERY OFFER

Enjoy FREE DELIVERY on orders within 3 km when your cart total is Rs 400 or more. Perfect for nearby birthdays, surprises, or last-minute celebration! Provide safe and careful delivery.

DELIVERY TIMING

Available Days: Monday to Sunday Delivery slots: 1pm to 5pm Please book in advance for custom & premium cakes (preferably 1–2 days ahead).

DELIVERY BEYOND 3KM

Delivery beyond 3 km will include additional charges based on distance. We ensure safe and careful delivery for all orders.
